About LIQUIDog

LIQUIDog (symbol: LQDog) is a playful, community‑driven memecoin on the Base network, with an entrepreneurial spirit. Every transaction carries a modest 1 % tax that is sent right back to the smart contract, slowly reducing the supply and helping each remaining token become a little more precious over time while maintaining a higher market cap.

Our vision is to foster a joyful pack that rallies around the idea of being financially liquid. As the project grows, we plan to explore dog‑focused charities and merchandise opportunities; including a platform allowing a user to create their own merchandise in minutes. The profits will be used to support the growth and buy back tokens. These possibilities are intentionally flexible as a like minded group of people with entrepreneurial spirit can accomplish almost anything.

Tokenomics

Total Supply

50 million LQDog tokens minted on the Base network — no future minting. 1 USDC liquidity pool and 1 ETH liquidity pool. Contract has no owner field.

Transaction Tax

1 % tax on every buy and sell, sent back to the contract to slowly create a deflationary effect without lowering the market cap.

Built on Base

Leveraging Base’s lightning‑fast, low‑fee infrastructure for seamless transactions.

Marketing Wallet

LIQUIDog's team controls a 15% marketing wallet to support ongoing promotion of the project. Possiblities include additional liquidity pools, engagement and milestone rewards, marketing, etc...
